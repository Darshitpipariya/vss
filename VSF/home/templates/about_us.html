About us

An unique initiative which connects Entrepreneurs, Investors and Business Experts.

Parul University and Its commitment towards fostering the spirit of Innovation and Entrepreneurship, is joining hands with industry and academia to make Vadodara, the Startup and Innovation Hub of Central Gujarat, with Vadodara Startup Studio.

Vadodara Startup Studio is an initiative of Parul Innovation And Entrepreneurship Research Centre, registered under Section 8 of the companies Act 2013,  to accelerate the Startup movement in central Gujarat.

Providing a vibrant ecosystem of Mentors, Industry connects, Quality, Infrastructure, Investors, and Network,  Vadodara Startup Studio will be the most active incubation and acceleration facility in central Gujarat.

The state of the art facility will have a seating capacity for 30+ startups & 40+ Individuals out of this facility and will bring in Industry Icons, Mentors, Investors under one roof
